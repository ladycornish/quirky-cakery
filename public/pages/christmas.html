It's Christmas....!

Every year I make a traditional cake for the family at Christmas time. This is where my love of cake decorating started and I look forward to it all year. I put a Christmas film on and take my time creating something fun.


A cake should be a wow factor, especially at Christmas. However after a while I was getting a bit bored of the usual toppings, so I began doing the unusual, much to the horror at times of my family.....as you will see!
