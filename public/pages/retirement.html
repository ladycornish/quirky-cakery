VERY Bespoke Retirements!

Oh I love these requests....as do my previous colleagues who were on the receiving end of my creations. I used to be a CSI & so when my favourite bosses retired I was asked to create their leaving cakes.


Needless to say these were not going to be run of the mill cakes, they be a little on the dark side.


I wasn't lying when I said no topic was off limits!

Crime Scene

My first Crime Scene Creation, a scene tent is over the body whilst CSI's process the crime scene aroud him.

CSI Dive Time

This CSI boss loves diving, so I encoroprated that into a crime scene. I am sending him in to a lake to retreive a sunken wrapped body! Of course!

Biggles In His Plane

The longest serving CSI required a special cake on his retirement. I had him flying in his by-plane over Southend Pier whilst his ex-collegues processed a scene.
